Panel Recruitment Methodology

Our rigorous recruitment process ensures high-quality, engaged panelists

  • Multi-Channel Recruitment

    We recruit panelists through targeted online advertising, social media campaigns, referral programs, and partnerships with trusted organizations.

  • Rigorous Verification

    Every panelist undergoes identity verification, email authentication, phone verification, and profile completeness checks before activation.

  • Profiling & Segmentation

    Comprehensive profiling captures demographics, psychographics, behavioral data, and professional information for precise targeting.

  • Engagement Programs

    Regular communication, fair incentives, transparent processes, and feedback mechanisms keep panelists active and engaged.

  • Continuous Quality Monitoring

    Real-time fraud detection, response quality checks, behavioral pattern analysis, and regular re-verification ensure data integrity.

  • Profile Updates

    Panelists are encouraged to update their profiles quarterly, ensuring fresh and accurate data for research matching.
